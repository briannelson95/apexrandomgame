Apex Randomizer

Updated for Season 7, add an extra challenge to your games of Apex Legends with the Apex Randomizer. Use this to generate two random weaponds with compatible attachments, legend and drop zones. The intended way to use the Apex Randomizer is by genetating a loadout and as you drop into a game, you can use any weapons you find until you find the weapons that have been assigned to you.
