Apex Legends Randomizer

Intended Play

The intended way to play is to generate a loadout (and/or) legend. As you drop into a match of APEX Legends, you can use any weapons you find until you are able to use the loadout that was generated.

Tested an alternate way to play, in which players were not allowed to use any other weapons than those that were generated. This means we could only fight with our fists, as you can imagine this was fairly difficult and in my opinion, made the challenge not fun at all. So take that for what it is and choose how you wish to play.
